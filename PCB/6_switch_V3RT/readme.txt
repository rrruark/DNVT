-The bottom side parts are optional (additional chassis ground to Pico Ground capacitors and uSD card holder)
-The Digikey BOM lists through hole parts (DC barrel jack and transformers) which must be manually assembled.
-The additional BOM lists parts not captured in the Eagle BOM export: the case, OLED screen, Front and back plates, screws, nuts, cable for screen, and 48V power supply.
